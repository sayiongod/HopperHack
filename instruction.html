Objective:
· The objective of the game is for the Detective and the Hostage to work together and guess what the object is within five attempts

Roles:
· Detective: The player who attempts to guess what the object is.
· Hostage: The player who provides hints to the Detective to help him/her guess the object.

Gameplay:
· Choose among yourself to who gets to be the detective and hostage.
· The Hostage must provide one hint per guess attempt by the Detective.
· Each hint must be at most ten characters.
· If the Detective guesses incorrectly, the Hostage can provide another hint.
· The Detective has five attempts to correctly guess the object.

Win Condition:
· If the Detective and the Hostage are able to correctly guess the object within five attempts, they win and the Hostage is rescued.

Lose Condition:
· If the Detective is unable to guess the object after five attempts, they lose and the Hostage remains captive.
